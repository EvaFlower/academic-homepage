\section{Biography}
My research primarily focuses on Generative AI, Controllable Generative Models, and AI for Bioscience. To date, I have published \textbf{20} academic papers, including those in top-tier AI/ML journals such as \textbf{JMLR}, \textbf{IEEE TPAMI} (2 papers), \textbf{IEEE TNNLS} (2 papers), and leading AI/ML conferences such as \textbf{ICLR} (2 papers), \textbf{IJCAI} (3 papers), \textbf{KDD}, and \textbf{CVPR}. I was honored with the \textbf{CPAL2024 Best Paper Award} and the \textbf{AI4SCI/NTCI2024 Best Poster Award}. As a lead PI or Co-I, I have led Singapore national-level research projects with total funding of approximately RMB 6.52 million, of which approximately RMB 2.74 million was allocated to me
Meanwhile, I actively promote the application of my research in fields such as enzyme engineering and structural biology, and have been granted \textbf{two Singapore patents}: (1) AI for enzyme design; and (2) AI for RNA structure evaluation, with corresponding international patents currently under application.
